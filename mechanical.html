Thinkpad Fan Upgrade

I upgraded my Thinkpad's cooling fan. The old one is aluminum with a single vent, the new one is copper with dual vents. I got the copper version from a similar model with a discrete GPU. After the replacement, the load temperatures dropped quite a bit, and I could run it fanless when just web-browsing. It also comes in useful when doing 3D rendering, as I can run the fan slower (with TPFanControl).

Since the new heatsink was meant for a discrete GPU, it didn't quite fit into my laptop. It only took a bit of dremelling to make it fit.
