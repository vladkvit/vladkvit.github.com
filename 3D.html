BMW M5 Model

3D, Blender, Artistic

This is a side project that I started back in first year of university. It's my first "real" 3D modelling work. I got reference photos and approximate blueprints for an E60 ('05-'10) and did the model from them.

BMW E24 6 Series Model

3D, Blender, Artistic

This is my second serious "car" project, started during my last year at university. The reference photos were harder to find, and the blueprints were quite approximate. I've learned how not to model with the first car, so this one's all polys and good topology.

Start-up Logo

3D, Blender, Artistic

I've always been fascinated by transferring flat 2D ideas onto 3D. I made my start-up's logo in 3D. Yes, I know that the 'o' is messed up.

The lighting is a large, warm area light on top, a warm top light for that extra shiny sparkle, 2 large cold side-lights, and one large bottom light

CS488 Final Project

3D, programming

This is my final project for the CS488 Graphics course. It has OpenGL rendering a few cubes with specular+diffuse shading and parallax mapping. Originally, the idea was to get a simulated windowing environment, but there's never enough time.
