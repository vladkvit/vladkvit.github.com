Start-up Logo

3D, Blender, Artistic

I've always been fascinated by transferring flat 2D ideas onto 3D. I made my start-up's logo in 3D. Yes, I know that the 'o' is messed up, that's the font's problem.

For the lighting, I used 5 area light sources. There's a large, warm light on top; a small, warm light on top for that extra shiny sparkle; 2 large cold lights on the sides; and one large, cold & dim light on the bottom. Post-pro: post-render light rebalancing, color curves, bit of color saturation & balance, denoising.
